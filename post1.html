5 DIY Projects for a Cozy Living Room

Turn your living room into a cozy haven with these easy DIY projects.

1. Handmade Throw Pillows

Add a pop of color and comfort to your living room with handmade throw pillows. Choose fabrics that complement your existing decor and create unique designs that reflect your personal style.

Buy Throw Pillows

2. DIY Wall Art

Create your own wall art using materials like canvas, paint, and stencils. This is a great way to add a personal touch to your living space and showcase your artistic talents.

Buy Wall Art Supplies

3. Upcycled Furniture

Give old furniture a new lease on life by upcycling. Sand down and repaint old pieces to match your decor, or add new hardware for a fresh look.

Buy Upcycling Supplies

4. Cozy Rugs

Add warmth and texture to your living room with cozy rugs. Choose a style and material that complements your decor and provides comfort underfoot.

Buy Cozy Rugs

5. Ambient Lighting

Set the mood with ambient lighting. Use string lights, floor lamps, and table lamps to create a warm and inviting atmosphere in your living room.
